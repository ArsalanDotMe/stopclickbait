Privacy Policy

This app does not gather ANY data from your facebook account or any other personal data.

When you click on the Open button, you will be taken to a page which will require you to authenticate with Google before you can add your explanations and upvote other explanations. The app uses this to avoid spam and stores no data from your Google account on its own servers. Only a profile ID which is specific to this app only, is stored per-user.
